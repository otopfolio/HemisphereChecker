How to Get Coordinates from Google Maps:

  1. On your computer, open Google Maps.
  2. Right-click the place or area on the map. This action will open a pop-up window.
  3. You can find your latitude and longitude in decimal format at the top of the pop-up window.
  4. To copy the coordinates automatically, left-click on the displayed latitude and longitude.

Tips:

  • For the coordinates to work properly use the decimal degree with decimals and comma
  • List latitude coordinates before longitude coordinates.
  • Ensure that the first number in your latitude coordinate is between -90 and 90.
  • Make sure that the first number in your longitude coordinate is between -180 and 180.
